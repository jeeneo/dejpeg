<p>This is not another "AI upscaler" but a compression artifact remover and denoiser using public models such as FBCNN and SCUNet</p>

<h2>Features:</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Remove compression artifacts</li>
  <li>Denoise</li>
  <li>Before/after view</li>
  <li>Fully offline</li>
</ul>

<h2>Limitations:</h2>
<ul>
  <li>processed locally, a fast device is recommended</li>
  <li>only standard image formats supported</li>
</ul>
