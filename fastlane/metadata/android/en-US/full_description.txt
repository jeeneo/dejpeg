<p><b>De<i>JPEG</i></b> is an Android app for removing jpeg compression artifacts and denoising images using <a href='https://github.com/jiaxi-jiang/FBCNN' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>FBCNN</a> and <a href='https://github.com/cszn/SCUNet' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>SCUNet</a>.</p>
<p><b>Features:</b></p>
<ul>
    <li>batch processing</li>
    <li>transparency re-mapping</li>
    <li>fully offline</li>
</ul>
<p>note: this is not a "super resolution AI upscaler", but a simple non-destructive way to clean up compressed/noisy images.</p>
<p><b>limitations:</b></p>
<ul>
    <li>processed locally, a device with 4gb of ram and a CPU with at least 4 threads is recommended</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Note: </b><b>De<i>JPEG</i></b> is not affiliated or related to Topaz <code>DEJPEG</code> or any other software/project.</p>
