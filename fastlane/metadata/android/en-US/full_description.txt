<p>De<i>JPEG</i> - an app for removing compression and noise from photos</p>

<h2>Features:</h2>
<ul>
  <li>remove compression artifacts</li>
  <li>denoise</li>
  <li>before/after view</li>
  <li>fully offline</li>
  <li>image descaling (see BRISQUE under in-app FAQs)</li>
</ul>

<p>This is not another "AI upscaler", but a compression artifact remover and denoiser using open-source weights such as FBCNN and SCUNet.</p>

<p>External models are required (links in-app)</p>

<h2>Limitation:</h2>
<ul>
  <li>processed locally, minimum 4gb ram and 4 threads recommended.</li>
  <li>large images might cause issues</li>
  <li>no GIF, TIFF or RAW support</li>
</ul>

<p>(note: De<i>JPEG</i> is not affiliated with Topaz DEJPEG or any other similarly named software/project)</p>
