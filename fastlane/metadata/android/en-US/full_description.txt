<p>Removes compression and noise from photos</p>

<p>Processed offline (no INTERNET permission) using downloadable ONNX models available <a href="https://codeberg.org/dryerlint/dejpeg/src/branch/main/models">here</a></p>

<p>Models: FBCNN (compression), SCUNet (noise), RMBG & U2NETP (background removal)</p>
