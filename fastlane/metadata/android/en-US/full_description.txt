<p><b>De<i>JPEG</i></b> is a free Android app for removing jpeg compression artifacts and denoising images with <a href='https://github.com/jiaxi-jiang/FBCNN' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>FBCNN</a> and <a href='https://github.com/cszn/SCUNet' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>SCUNet</a> with mobile-compatible models.</p>
<p><br><b>Features:</b></p>
<ul>
   <li>batch processing</li>
   <li>transparancy re-mapping (experimental)</li>
   <li>exporting as BMP or PNG</li>
   <li>free and no internet needed</li>
</ul>
<p><b>limitations:</b></p>
<ul>
   <li>processed on your phone (no gpu), I'd recommend a device with 4gb of ram or more</li>
   <li>input images above 1200px will be processed in chunks</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Note: </b><b>De<i>JPEG</i></b> is not affiliated or related to Topaz's <code>DEJPEG</code> or any other software/project.</p>
